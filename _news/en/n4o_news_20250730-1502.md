A **comprehensive report on the joint workshop** of the NFDI4Memory, Text+ and NFDI4Objects consortia on "**High-quality metadata in editions**" is now available on the Text+ website: <https://textplus.hypotheses.org/13390>. 

The workshop took place in March at the [annual conference Digital Humanities in the German-speaking world](https://dhd2025.dig-hum.de/) in Bielefeld. 

Further information on **NFDI4Objects at the DHd** can be found in the related [activity](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/activities/n4o_activity_20250221-1122/) on our website.
