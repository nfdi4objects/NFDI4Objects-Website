The Community Cluster ‘Digital Provenance Research’ invites all interested parties to the next meeting on **Thursday, 26 June 2025 at 1 pm** online via Zoom.

Zoom meeting

<https://dainst-org.zoom.us/j/93018071726?pwd=U2RoOGhldmR0a2FWT3JzT0pBVmxoZz09>

Registration is not necessary.

On the agenda:

* News from NFDI4Objects on digital provenance research
* Planning of our meeting at the Community Meeting in Bochum (end of September)
* Outlook on activities in 2025/26

Due to the large number of parallel events, we will not be giving a thematic presentation this time - and are instead looking forward to an open exchange: What are you currently working on? What projects are on the agenda? What are your common interests?

Come along - for a digital coffee and good conversation!

You can find more information about the cluster on the [portal](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc5_provenance_research/).
