On 14 and 15 October 2025, the [**DiHMa.Lab** of Freie Universität Berlin](https://www.ada.fu-berlin.de/ada-labs/dihma-lab/index.html) in cooperation with **ZIB** invites you to a joint workshop of the **[MaRDI](https://www.mardi4nfdi.de/about/mission)** and **NFDI4Objects** consortia at Freie Universität Berlin. The interdisciplinary workshop will bring together different perspectives from mathematics and object-based humanities around the topic of ‘*Objects and Methods*’. The aim is to bring approaches, tools and questions from both disciplines into dialogue, to make interfaces visible and to discuss common methodological challenges. The workshop language is English.

The preliminary programme is still being updated:

*14.10.2025 (10 am - 5 pm)*

* Ediathon: ‘Object meets Maths: Wikidata as a Knowledge Hub’ - Florian Thiery (LEIZA), Anja Gerber (KSW) (10:00 am - 12:00 pm)
* Lunch on a self-pay basis in the [cafeteria](https://www.stw.berlin/mensen/einrichtungen/freie-universit%C3%A4t-berlin/mensa-fu-pharmazie.html) (12 - 1 pm)
* Icebreaker and introduction round (1:00 - 1:45 pm)
* Presentation of the NFDI consortia (2:00 - 2:45 pm)
