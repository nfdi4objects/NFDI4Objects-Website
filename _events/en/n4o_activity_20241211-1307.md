On 7 February 2025, we will be offering **online counselling for students and doctoral candidates** on all **questions relating to research data management for theses** for the first time. 

A total of eight **slots of 30 minutes each** will be offered for booking during the period from 12 noon to 4 pm. You can book a counselling slot via the [DFN appointment scheduler](https://terminplaner6.dfn.de/b/0ab6b5b92523fd7b8f07d74e5b8b4966-1008504). 

If you have any questions about this counselling service, please contact our [helpdesk](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/helpdesk/).

We would be delighted if you would share the offer with potentially interested parties. Thank you very much!
