## Project Description

The project "Exploring the RDM Landscape in Museums and Collections" systematically examines existing approaches to cataloging collection objects.

## Goal

Creating a structured foundation for further necessary work in the areas of standardization, networking, interoperability, and publication of research data from collection contexts.

## Focus Areas

- Systematic documentation of existing cataloging approaches
- Analysis of the RDM landscape in museums
- Identification of challenges and best practices
- Development of recommendations for collection institutions

## Task Area

This project is assigned to **Task Area 2: Collecting** and focuses on the collection and documentation aspect of research data management.
