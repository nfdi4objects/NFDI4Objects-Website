Our 2nd NFDI4Objects Community Meeting took place from 25 to 27 September 2024 at the Leibniz Centre for Archaeology in Mainz and Mainz University of Applied Sciences. We would like to thank all the organisers for the successful event and all 185 visitors for their input and participation.

You couldn't be there in person? No problem: [Here](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20240806-1114/) you will find a detailed review with many photos of the Community Meeting 2024. Enjoy reading!
