On the afternoon of 23 January, the online workshop **Uncertainties and Vagueness in Provenance Data and their Modelling**, organised by the Community Cluster [Provenance Research](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc5_provenance_research/), took place. As part of the [Trail 2.4](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/trails/digital_provenance_research_methodologies_concepts_and_name_authority_files/) work programme, various aspects of the topic were to be examined in keynote speeches and a broad discussion.

The workshop focussed on the spectrum of gaps, vagueness and uncertainties in provenance data. In addition, the needs in dealing with these and possibly derivable recommendations for mapping were discussed. Modelling options and practical examples were also discussed.

You can find a more detailed report [here.](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20250123-1246/)
