## About the Workshop

This two-day workshop is aimed at researchers from archaeology and related object sciences who wish to deepen their knowledge of research data management.

## Programme

### Day 1: Data Capture and Documentation
- Best practices for field documentation
- Digital recording methods
- Metadata standards

### Day 2: Laboratory Data and Experimental Archaeology
- Documentation of laboratory analyses
- Instrument parameters and reproducibility
- FAIR principles in practice

## Target Audience

- Archaeologists in field research
- Laboratory scientists
- Data managers
- Early career researchers

## Registration

Participation is limited to 30 people. Registration deadline is March 1, 2025.
