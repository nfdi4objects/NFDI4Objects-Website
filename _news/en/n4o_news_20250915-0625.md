The workshop of the Community Cluster ‘[Objects as Inscription Carriers](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc8_objects_as_information_carriers/)’ took place on 28 April 2025 at the Archäologisches Zentrum Berlin and virtually and brought together experts from archaeology, art history, Egyptology, numismatics and digital humanities, including from the NFDI consortia Text+ and 4Memory. The aim of this event, which followed an initial workshop in October 2024, was to discuss current projects and challenges in the digital indexing and modelling of inscription carriers, particularly with regard to standard data, interoperable metadata models and ontologies, as well as the creation of synergetic strategies in various projects and consortia. [The report and further information](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20250422-0737/#looking-back) can be found in the related activity on our website.
