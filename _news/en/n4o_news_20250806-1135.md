***You can now register for our third Community Meeting from 30 September to 2 October in Bochum.***

In a few months it will be time again: our Community Meeting will take place for the third time. This year, NFDI4Objects invites you to the German Mining Museum in Bochum from 30 September to 2 October.

There is no participation fee for our Community Meeting. However, registration for the conference and workshops is required. **The registration deadline for the Community Meeting is 19 September 2025**.

[Register here now!](https://reg.nfdi4objects.net/N4O/CM2025/)

There will be a varied programme again this year. In exciting talks and workshops you will learn more about the work of our consortium, while our poster session and our Ignite Talks offer space for your research and stories about research data management. And in 2025, there will again be plenty of opportunities to socialise with others from our community over snacks and drinks.

[Click here for the programme.](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/activities/n4o_activity_20250324-0956/#programm)

Please note that there may be changes to the programme at short notice. These changes will be continuously updated on our event page.

**[All information about the 3rd NFDI4Objects Community Meeting this year in Bochum can be found here.](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/activities/n4o_activity_20250324-0956/)**
