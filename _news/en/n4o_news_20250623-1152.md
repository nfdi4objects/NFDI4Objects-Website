The Generaldirektion Kulturelles Erbe Rhineland-Palatinate (GDKE) - Digitisation Office - is seeking to fill a **full-time (39 hours per week)** project position as a (geo)computer scientist (m/f/d) **until 31.12.2027** as soon as possible. The position is graded according to **E 13 TV-L**. **Mainz, Göttingen or Berlin** can be offered as **places of employment**. 

Tasks include the programming of interfaces, web applications and databases, the provision of (geo)basic services and tools for geodata processing, and the development of machine- and human-readable interfaces and digital web services for the restoration and conservation community as well as for analysing manufacturing and production processes.

Here you can find the [job advertisement](https://gdke.rlp.de/wer-wir-sind/stabsstellen/personal-stellenangebote/detail/geo-informatiker-in-m-w-d-2). The **application deadline** is **14 July 2025**.

We look forward to receiving your application!
