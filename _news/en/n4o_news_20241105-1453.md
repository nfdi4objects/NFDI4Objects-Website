Following many requests from the CAA community, the organising committee of the **Computer Applications and Quantitative Methods in Archaeology (CAA) International Conference 2025** in Athens is **extending** the submission deadline for the current **Call for Papers**.

Proposals for contributions to the sessions can be submitted until **24 November 2024**.

The complete call can be found on the [CAA International website](https://2025.caaconference.org/2024/10/28/announcement-call-for-papers-workshops-extension/). Further information on the event can be found [here](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20241016-0928/).
