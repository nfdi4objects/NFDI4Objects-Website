The inaugural meeting of the new FDM Support Community Cluster will take place on **January 19, 2026, at 10 a.m.** 

The cluster is aimed at anyone involved in FDM consulting who would like to discuss consulting needs and the consortium's offerings with representatives from NFDI4Objects. 

**Zoom link:**  
<https://hs-mainz-de.zoom-x.de/j/69165433661?pwd=HnpaxCTYQAAeAjztRnwpbu2hvkHSvE.1>  

**Meeting ID:** 691 6543 3661  

**Access code:** 889016

In the first meeting, we would like to clarify goals, tasks, and organizational framework conditions, explore initial content priorities, and exchange ideas about expectations and opportunities for active participation.

**Preliminary agenda**

1. Welcome and, if necessary, introductions

2. Presentation of the objectives of the Community Cluster

3. Exchange on expectations and needs

4. Focus topic: FAIRe FDM training and self-learning materials in NFDI4Objects

5. Planning of further dates

6. Miscellaneous

**Registration is not necessary. We look forward to your participation.**
