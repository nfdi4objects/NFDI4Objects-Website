The Community Cluster ‘*Protected Heritage Sites*’ invites all interested parties to the next meeting on **Monday, 18 August 2025 from 11:00-13:00 online** via Zoom. In this session we will focus on the topic of "**Dating**". Everyone is cordially invited and encouraged to bring examples from their own institutions and data systems as well as invite colleagues.

Questions to be discussed include:

* How should dating be specified? Absolute or relative, from/to?
* Can the dating be specified using a (multi-level) thesaurus?
* Is a further field required for additional, supplementary dating information?
