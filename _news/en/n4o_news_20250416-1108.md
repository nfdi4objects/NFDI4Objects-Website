The meeting will take place online on Wednesday, **April 30, 2025, 10:00 - 12:00**. 

The chairs of the NFDI4Objects Temporary Working Group “Environment Analysis Collecting”, Felix Schäfer and Johannes Schäffer, will present the overall conclusion of the working group with recommendations for the design and further development of indexing, digitization and research data management in museums and collections. These recommendations are intended to help shape the future work of the NFDI4Objects consortium and appeal to everyone working in collections and museums in Germany. We look forward to broad participation and will provide ample opportunity for discussion in small groups!

Registration is not necessary, you can simply join via link to Zoom: <https://hu-berlin.zoom-x.de/j/63390806995?pwd=U3qEpqaQ6bE7lhFaKcL1iqq3cZFofU.1>  

Further information about the cluster can be found on the [portal ](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc6_collection_management/)of the N4O website.
