At the end of 2024, **Base4NFDI** is organising a **roadshow** in which all **NFDI basic services** will be presented. Each service will be presented for 10 minutes. This will be followed by 5 minutes for discussion. Further information can be found on the [Base4NFDI website](https://base4nfdi.de/news-events/events/services-roadshow-by-base4nfdi-12-2024).

## Registration

Registration is required for participation. After registering, you will be sent the access data for the virtual room by e-mail.

## Programme

The roadshow will take place online via Zoom on **4 December from 2pm**. You can find the programme below.

* 14:00: Welcome by Base4NFDI 
* 14:05: IAM4NFDI
* 14:20: PID4NFDI
* 14:35: TS4NFDI
* 14:50: Jupyter4NFDI
* 15:05: Break (approx. 10 minutes)
* 15:15: DMP4NFDI
* 15:30: KGI4NFDI
* 3:45 pm: nfdi.software
* 16:00: RDMTraining4NFDI

All interested parties are welcome. Base4NFDI is looking forward to your active participation!

Do you have any **questions** about the roadshow or the Basic Services? Then please send an e-mail to **base4nfdi-office@lists.nfdi.de**. Thank you very much.
