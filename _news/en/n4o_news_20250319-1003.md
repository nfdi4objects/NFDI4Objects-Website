The Institute of Architecture at Mainz University of Applied Sciences in cooperation with the GDKE, ICOMOS Germany and CIPA Heritage Documentation invite you to the conference ‘Heritage BIM in Monument Preservation - Innovative Strategies for Conservation and Monitoring’, a kick-off event of the project ‘Heritage Building Information Modelling (HBIM)’ for the sustainable preservation of UNESCO World Heritage Sites using the example of the Worms Synagogue.

Date: 27th-28th of March 2025

Location: LUX - Pavilion of Mainz University of Applied Sciences, Ludwigsstr. 2, 55116 Mainz

[Link to the Website](https://architekturinstitut.hs-mainz.de/projects/HBIM-Worms-Conference) with registration and program

Our consortium will be represented with a contribution on ‘Bridging Two Worlds - Linked Open Data meets HBIM - Heritage Management of Large Historic Buildings with the MonArch-System’ by Tobias Arera-Rütenik and Alexander Stenzer on Friday, 28 March from 11:00 to 11:30am.
