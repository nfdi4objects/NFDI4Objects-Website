We cordially invite you to the presentation ‘**The use of BARTOC and Cocoda for the NFDI**’ with **Jakob Voß** from the Verbundzentrale Göttingen on **Wednesday, 4 June 2025 from 1 pm to 2 pm** as part of the CC Authority Files and Community-driven Vocabularies. 

The presentation will take place via Zoom: <https://dainst-org.zoom.us/j/99974415036?pwd=UVc3SmJ4Y01HaXdEc3Y3M2lzOFpMUT09> 

Meeting ID: 999 7441 5036, Passcode: 353053

The [Basic Register of Thesauri, Ontologies & Classifications (BARTOC)](https://bartoc.org/) is a database for listing various knowledge organisation systems (classifications, standard data, thesauri...) and related registers. One of its aims is to achieve greater visibility and to enable searchability and comparability.

[Cocoda](https://coli-conc.gbv.de/cocoda/) is a web application for the creation and management of mappings between different knowledge organisation systems and is maintained as part of the [coli-conc project](https://coli-conc.gbv.de/) at the [GBV's Central Office (VZG)](https://www.gbv.de/).

Further information on the [Community Cluster Authority Files and Community-driven Vocabularies](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc3_authority_and_norm_data/) can be found in the portal on our website.
