We invite all interested parties to the **3rd meeting** of the NFDI4Objects Community Cluster ‘Denkmal-, Maßnahmen- und Schutzflächen / Protected Heritage Sites’ on **12 May 2025 from 10:00-12:00 am**.

[Zoom access](https://dainst-org.zoom.us/j/93152008530?pwd=NkJ2MzVRU1IxaDl2WXBDVEdQSHcvZz09)

[](https://dainst-org.zoom.us/j/93152008530?pwd=NkJ2MzVRU1IxaDl2WXBDVEdQSHcvZz09)Information on the objectives of the cluster can be found on the [portal](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/portal/ccs/cc-26-denkmalfl%C3%A4chen-ma%C3%9Fnahmenfl%C3%A4chen-schutzfl%C3%A4chen/) of the N4O website.

During the meeting, we will continue to work on draft data models for the areas of cultural heritage objects, measures, protected areas and citizen science. These are intended to serve as a standard for corresponding data sets for archaeology, monument preservation and building research in order to enable nationwide compatible data exchange.  

The drafts and all information on previous meetings of the cluster are available on [OSF](https://osf.io/pwzg4/wiki/home/).

We look forward to a lively exchange and your input. 

Please feel free to invite other interested colleagues and distribute the invitation via your channels.    

If you would like to stay informed about the work of the cluster, please subscribe to our [mailing list](https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/n4o_cc_protected_heritage_sites).
