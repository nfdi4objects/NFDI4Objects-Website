Thanks to the European Archaeological Association for such a great conference in Rome last week.

NFDI4Objects was present at several sessions on 3D data within archaeology, research data management and research software engineering for archaeological sciences. 

Many thanks to our speakers Florian Thiery, Fabian Fricke, Julian Hollaender, Karsten Tolle, Moritz Menenga, Fabian Riebschläger and Carina Knappe for their fascinating insights on different tools and services developed by NFDI4Objects for and by our community.

You want to learn more about our tools and services? Then join us at our [2nd NFDI4Objects Community Meeting from 25 to 27 September in Mainz](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20240806-1114/)! Registration ends on 13 September.
