The launch of the academy project [ImagNum](https://www.imagines-nummorum.eu/en), in which our co-applicant [Staatliche Museen zu Berlin](https://www.smb.museum/en/home/) is significantly involved, will be celebrated on **Wednesday, 9 April 2025, 5:00pm**. The event will take place in the Lise-Meitner-Saal, Unter den Linden 8, 10117 Berlin. The NFDI4Objects community is cordially invited. Further information can be found [here](https://www.imagines-nummorum.eu/en/news). 

Please register here: <https://www.bbaw.de/veranstaltungen/veranstaltung-was-wollen-und-koennen-muenzen>.
