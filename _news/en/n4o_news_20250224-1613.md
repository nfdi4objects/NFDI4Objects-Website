In the new academy project ‘[Imagines Nummorum Graecorum](https://www.imagines-nummorum.eu/en)’ at the Berlin-Brandenburg Academy of Sciences and Humanities, there is currently a job advertisement for students of ancient numismatics, classical archaeology and other ancient studies. 

We are looking for 2 student assistants **for a minimum of 42 hours/month with immediate effect and for a minimum of 12 months**.

Tasks include photographing ancient Greek coins, recording basic coin data in the Interactive Catalogue of the Münzkabinett Berlin (IKMK) and describing coins and coin types in the Corpus Nummorum and the IKMK.

Here you can find the [job advertisement](https://numismatische-kommission.de/fileadmin/user_upload/Nk/neuigkeiten/2025_02_16_Ausschreibung_AV_01_2025_SHK_ImagNum.pdf). The application deadline is 10 March 2025.
