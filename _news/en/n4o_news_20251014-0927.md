Kristina Fischer, Anja Gerber and Frank von Hagel represented our consortium at FORGE2025 (Research Data in the Humanities) in Rostock with several contributions (workshop, presentation and poster). Anja Gerber and Kristina Fischer were also on site on Thursday during the breaks and lectures in the NFDI slot as contact persons for questions about N4O.

The abstracts, presentation slides and posters in which N4O was involved are now published on Zenodo. An overview can be found in the corresponding [activity on our website](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20250804-1651/).
