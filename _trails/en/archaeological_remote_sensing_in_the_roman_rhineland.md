## Description

The aim is to develop and describe generally applicable workflows and standards for the processing, evaluation and long-term storage of remote sensing data and geophysical sensor information in a common geoinformation system. Roman sites west of the Rhine serve as an example, taking into account existing workflows and guidelines.
