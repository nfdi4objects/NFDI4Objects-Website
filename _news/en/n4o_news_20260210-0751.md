Since 2018, more than 700 FactGrid accounts have been created. With the continuous growth of the community and the increasing establishment of FactGrid as an institution in the field of historical data, a new service has been introduced: the FactGrid Community Mailing List.

The FactGrid Community Mailing List offers a flexible and user-friendly way to exchange information within the community, as users can subscribe and unsubscribe at any time. The clear user interface facilitates access and provides, among other things, a direct link to the community.

This mailing list can be used to ask other members specific questions, for example about current problems or about certain features and functions that are needed. It is also possible to ask whether such features already exist or can be created by other community members.

The community brings together experts from various fields who have often already dealt with and solved similar data-related issues and can therefore offer valuable support.

Link to mailing list: <https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/factgrid-community>
