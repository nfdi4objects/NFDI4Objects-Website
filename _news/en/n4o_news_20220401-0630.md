On 16 June, a joint conference entitled ‘**FAIR data and digital infrastructures for archaeology. Shaping spaces for action together, identifying potential**’ organised by DGUF and NFDI4Objects will take place in Frankfurt.

Presentations or discussion/debate contributions can still be submitted until **19 April 2022**.

Further information can be found here: <https://dguf.de/tagungen/aktuelle-tagung>
