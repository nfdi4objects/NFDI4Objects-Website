At the end of January 2025, the Coordination Office visited our co-applicant in Task Area 6, the Klassik Stiftung Weimar, to discuss the annual planning for 2025. Among other things, the internal evaluation and the consortium's interim report were prepared in small groups in the beautiful atmosphere of the [Goethe and Schiller Archive](https://www.klassik-stiftung.de/goethe-und-schiller-archiv/). With many thanks to the Directorate of Museums and the cross-sectional Directorate of Digital Transformation and Innovation Management, the [Goethe National Museum](https://www.klassik-stiftung.de/goethe-nationalmuseum/) was first visited analogue and then virtually via the [BMBF-funded research project Goethe-Live-3D](https://www.klassik-stiftung.de/goethe-nationalmuseum/goethe-live-3d/).
