The first meeting of the Community Cluster Digital Provenance Research in 2025 will take place on **Thursday, 13 March 2025, starting at 13:00**. 

The topic of this meeting will be the annual planning for 2025, and there will also be a report from the working groups in NFDI4Objects that deal with digital provenance research.

A warm invitation is extended to all interested parties!
