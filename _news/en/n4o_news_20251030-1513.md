We cordially invite you to the inaugural meeting of the NFDI4Objects Community Cluster Service Portfolio Development on 6 November 2025 from 4:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.

Zoom access details: [https://hs-mainz-de.zoom.us/j/61662169537?pwd=VTdqeXNyTTFCNVNxOERmOW4zUVlsdz09](https://hs-mainz-de.zoom.us/j/61662169537?pwd=VTdqeXNyTTFCNVNxOERmOW4zUVlsdz09)

Meeting ID: 616 6216 9537, passcode: 611163

The Community Cluster is an interest group that develops quality standards for the certification of services within NFDI4Objects and adapts them to current developments. In consultation with the community, we also develop practical implementation and workflows for the defined specifications, taking into account all stakeholders involved in the process.

The first step in the CC's work involves defining service-specific quality standards for the NFDI4Objects service portfolio as a basis for the work process in line with the FAIR/CARE/TRUST principles within the consortium. This will be published as a Commons contribution and will thus be available to the community as an NFDI4Objects standard.

The [proposal with more detailed information on the cluster's objectives is published on Zenodo](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17473685).

At the inaugural meeting, we would like to briefly introduce the topic and objectives of our CCs and then move straight on to discussing the work packages.

Following the inaugural meeting, there will be further meetings at regular intervals.

Please subscribe to the following mailing list if you would like to stay informed about the work of the cluster: [https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/n4o_cc_diensteportfolio_management](https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/n4o_cc_diensteportfolio_management).
