Regularly, every first Friday of the month, from 1:00 p.m. to 1:45 p.m., the “Lunch & Learn on Digital Art History” takes place online – the monthly digital lunch break for questions and topics related to digital art history.

On Friday, March 6, 2026, Anja Gerber (KSW, NFDI4Objects) will present NFDI4Objects, the research data infrastructure for material legacies from human and environmental history.

After a short introduction (5 min), Anja Gerber will present the keynote speech (10 min) for the session. This will be followed by a short discussion (15 min) on the topic, after which questions can be answered and tips given (15 min).


**ZOOM**: [https://us02web.zoom.us/j/84286572737 pwd=VrT2D61m8S2g1CCD6QlD3qVVgy8yCL.1](https://us02web.zoom.us/j/84286572737?pwd=VrT2D61m8S2g1CCD6QlD3qVVgy8yCL.1)


Due to the holidays in April and May, Lunch&Learn will not take place in April and will therefore resume on May 8, 2026.

The working group's activities are discussed at the regular meeting (first Wednesday of the month, 8 a.m.), and contributions can be made in the working group's Slack channel.

Further information on the Digital Art History working group can be found here: 
<https://digitale-kunstgeschichte.de/>
