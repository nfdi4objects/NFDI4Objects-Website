**CAA Germany** and **NFDI4Objects** cordially invite all students and researchers working on the material heritage of human and environmental history to an interactive webcast:

**Date: 14 November 2025
Time: 3:00 – 4:00 pm
Location: Online via Zoom**

NFDI4Objects develops practical solutions for **research data management (RDM)** on material heritage from around three million years of human and environmental history.

The webcast offers you the opportunity to talk directly to the experts from NFDI4Objects and ask your questions about research data management, for example:

* Which file formats are suitable for long-term archiving?
* How do I deal with copyright-protected materials?
* Which file formats are suitable for my project?
* How do I document excavation and prospection results, find data or 3D models in such a way that they remain reusable and archivable?
* Which repositories are suitable for publishing my research data?
* Which controlled vocabularies are relevant for my research questions?

Whether you already have specific challenges or would first like to get an overview of the topic - your participation is welcome.

### Submit questions and help shape the content

Submit your questions by 7 November 2025 and help tailor the webcast to the needs of the community.

Even if you don't have any questions of your own, it's still worth participating: you'll find out what topics other colleagues are interested in and receive practical tips for working with research data.

You can submit your questions using [this form provided by CAA e.V.](https://forms.gle/JkLNJsiytnZKZ8dVA).

### Get to know the helpdesk and the NFDI4Objects team

Take this opportunity to get to know the NFDI4Objects helpdesk – the central point of contact for questions about FDM. Meet the people behind NFDI4Objects and find out how they can support you in your research.

We look forward to your participation and the opportunity to exchange ideas!
