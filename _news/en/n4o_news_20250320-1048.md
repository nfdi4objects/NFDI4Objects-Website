On February 14, 2025, the Community Cluster (CC) “Authority Files and Community-driven Vocabularies” was approved by the Steering Committee in NFDI4Objects. Authority files and terminologies are a focal point for describing the content of the consortium's research data. The focus of the CC is on the (further) development of various authority files and terminologies (in this context, this includes term lists, glossaries, thesauri and vocabularies) as well as discussions on the content.

The first meeting of the CC will take place on **Thursday, April 3, 2025, 2 to 3pm** via [Zoom](https://dainst-org.zoom.us/j/99974415036?pwd=UVc3SmJ4Y01HaXdEc3Y3M2lzOFpMUT09).

Further information on the cluster can be found in the [portal](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc3_authority_and_norm_data/).
