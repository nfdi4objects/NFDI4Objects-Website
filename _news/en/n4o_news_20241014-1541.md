The **[Data Competence Centre for Scientific University Collections, SODa](https://sammlungen.io/de)**, invites you to the first Barcamp in Berlin. From **14 to 16 October 2024**, the event will take place in presence on the premises of Humboldt University. 

**Who is the SODa Barcamp for?**

For anyone who works professionally and scientifically in and with collections and their objects. Anyone can take part, there are no preconditions or minimum requirements!

**What exactly is a Barcamp?**

At a Barcamp, the participants determine the programme themselves. We decide together on site which topics we would like to discuss and how (discussions, work on data, workshops, excursions, etc.). A barcamp lives from and for the interests of everyone who attends! What SODa offers: Many of our employees are on site and are happy to contribute their ideas and expertise!
