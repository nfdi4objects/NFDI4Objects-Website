The next meeting of the Community Cluster Konservierung und Restaurierung / Conservation Science will take place virtually on May 6, 2025, from 2:00 pm to 4:00 pm. Everyone who deals with research data in the field of conservation – or wants to do so in the future – is invited.

On the agenda:

* Insights into the results of our survey on the handling of research data in the field of conservation 
* Presentation of a first draft of a (content-related) minimum data set for the documentation of conservation  measures
* Open discussion and professional exchange

[Link to the virtual meeting via Zoom](https://dainst-org.zoom.us/j/93152008530?pwd=NkJ2MzVRU1IxaDl2WXBDVEdQSHcvZz09)

Meeting ID: 931 5200 8530  \
Passcode: 017928

Further topic suggestions are welcome and can be sent to Nathaly Witt until **April 30, 2025:** nathaly.witt@leiza.de. 

We look forward to your participation and a stimulating exchange!

Please also forward the invitation to interested colleagues.

All further information on the cluster can be found on the N4O website [portal](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc14_conservation_sciences/).
