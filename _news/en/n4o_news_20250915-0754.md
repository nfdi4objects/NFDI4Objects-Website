The **Berlin Autumn Meeting of Museum Documentation** will take place in Berlin from 6 to 8 October. The meeting will focus on **AI and research data management**. NFDI4Objects will be represented with several contributions at the Berlin Autumn Meeting of Museum Documentation. [](https://www.museumsbund.de/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/fg-doku25-programm-herbsttagung.pdf)

[Here you can find the programme](https://www.museumsbund.de/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/fg-doku25-programm-herbsttagung.pdf) for the Berlin Autumn Meeting of Museum Documentation 2025. 

[You can still register via the google form.](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScsQl3-HE9t0OhphslcUrsIvuaomvrSCkiW_Q6czqh43X-JEw/viewform) If you wish to receive a confirmation, you must enter an e-mail address. 

Organiser: FG Documentation at the German Museum Association e.V. - Institute for museum research, Staatliche Museen zu Berlin, digiS Berlin

Place and time: Zuse-Institut-Berlin, Takustraße 7; from 6 to 8 October 2025, Presence and hybrid

Contact: [fg-dokumentation@museumsbund.de](mailto:fg-dokumentation@museumsbund.de) 

The event will take place on site and online and participation is free of charge. Further information can be found on the website of the [German Museum Association](https://www.museumsbund.de/termine/herbsttagung-2025-der-fachgruppe-dokumentation/).
