The work programme of NFDI4Objects has been enriched by a Temporary Working Group! The **TWG ‘Status quo of the material qualities of written artefacts in epigraphic tools and services: checklist and analysis’** was approved by the Steering Committee last week.

The TWG is still looking for **interested parties** to participate until **18 December 2024**. Are you interested? Then please get in touch with Christoph Klose (C.Klose@smb.spk-berlin.de). We look forward to your active participation!

You can find more information on the objectives and the TWG proposal in our [portal](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/portal/twgs/status-quo-des-schrifttr%C3%A4gers-in-epigraphischen-tools-und-services-checkliste-und-analyse/).

Would you like to find out more about the Temporary Working Group format? [Here ](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/about/consortium/#temporary-working-groups)we explain what is behind it.
