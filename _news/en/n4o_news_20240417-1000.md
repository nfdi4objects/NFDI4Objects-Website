Students, researchers and teachers collect research data in projects and papers every day. The usability of this data often ends with the end of the project or submission of the term paper. It cannot be found or accessed by other researchers and is rarely documented in such a way that it can be used in other research scenarios.

NFDI4Objects wants to change that! Research data should become [FAIR](https://www.go-fair.org/fair-principles/) - Findable, Accessible, Interoperable and Reusable - and the data should thus be given a [life cycle](https://forschungsdaten.info/themen/informieren-und-planen/datenlebenszyklus/). We support this process with our dataship.

NFDI4Objects is offering a total of **four dataships** as a **three-month fellowship**, which involves the preparation of datasets by their creators in accordance with the FAIR criteria for a fee.

**Application deadline: 17 May 2024**

You can find more information about the Dataship and the application process [here](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20240701-0632/#call-for-proposals).

We wish you every success with your application!
