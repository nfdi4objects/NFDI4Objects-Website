The Community Cluster ‘[Conservation Science](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc14_conservation_sciences/)’ cordially invites all interested parties to the next meeting on **Wednesday, 17 September 2025, from 10:00-12:00** online via Zoom.

The meeting will focus on the revised version of the **minimum data set for the documentation of conservation and restoration measures**. This has been further developed based on the feedback received since the last meeting. To illustrate the content, an interactive **mock-up page** was created that simulates an example input mask with the proposed data fields. In addition, a **metadata thesaurus** is available, which explains the terms used in more detail and can be accessed directly via the interface.

We invite all participants to familiarise themselves with both resources in advance and to check their applicability in their own documentation context. In the session, the developments will be presented in detail and there will also be room for questions, feedback and discussion.
