On 7 November 2024, NFDI4Objects invites all regional employees and stakeholders from NFDI consortia, research data management initiatives, data competence centres and the promotion of young researchers based in Berlin-Brandenburg to a network meeting in Berlin. The programme for the face-to-face meeting has now been published.

All information about the event including the programme can be found [here](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20241009-1137/).
