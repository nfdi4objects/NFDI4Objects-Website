From **13 to 15 November 2024**, the **FAIRagro Plenary** will take place in Berlin-Dahlem at the **Julius Kühn-Institute**.  We are very pleased that **NFDI4Objects** is represented by its Managing Director, Dr. Christin Keller, at the Lightnight Talks and the Panel Discussion.

The **Lightning Talks** will take place from 3:15 pm to 4:30 pm in presence. The topic of the talks is **‘Insights into other NFDI consortia’**. Christin Keller will present the NFDI4Objects consortium in this context. The **Panel Discussion** will take place in hybrid form from 5 pm.

All information on the programme can be found on the [FAIRagro website](https://fairagro.net/en/dont-miss-out-fairagro-plenary-from-november-13-15-2024-in-berlin/).
