We are pleased to be able to offer a new service for our community: On 7 February 2025, the online consultation for students and doctoral candidates will take place for the first time. 

You can easily book a 30-minute slot via the [DFN scheduler](https://terminplaner6.dfn.de/b/0ab6b5b92523fd7b8f07d74e5b8b4966-1008504) and then ask all your questions about research data management for your theses.

You can find more information in the [corresponding activity](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20241211-1307/).
