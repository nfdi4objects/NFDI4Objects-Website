The project **[Digital Competences in the Archaeological Disciplines (DiKopA)](https://dikopa.net/)**, which is based at the Classical Archaeology Department of TU Darmstadt, is organising a workshop from 19 to 20 May 2025 with lectures on digitisation, digital methods in teaching and research data management in archaeology.

The project is funded by the Foundation for Innovation in University Teaching and is being organised in cooperation with NFDI4Objects.
