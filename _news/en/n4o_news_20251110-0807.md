The Community Cluster ‘Protected Heritage Sites’ invites all interested parties to its next meeting on Tuesday, 11 November 2025, from 11:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m., online via Zoom. 

At this meeting, we will finalise the data model for cultural heritage objects, discuss how to proceed, and then turn our attention to the citizen science model.

[Zoom meeting](https://zoom.us/j/91094521138?pwd=zJLwXZetdBcpMpssA9RpZPVCRFUWDP.1)

Further information on the cluster can be found on the [portal](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/portal/ccs/cc-26-denkmalfl%C3%A4chen-ma%C3%9Fnahmenfl%C3%A4chen-schutzfl%C3%A4chen/).

Registration is not necessary. We look forward to your participation!
