## Description

This TWG is still in preparation.

Here you will soon find all information about the TWG . If you are curious and interested in participating in our TWG, please contact our [Helpdesk](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/portal/helpdesk/).

## Focus

The focus is on standardized metadata for instrument configurations in analytical methods such as:
- Optical microscopy
- X-ray fluorescence
- Raman spectroscopy


## Goals

The goal is to develop a metadata profile for instrument parameters that are significant for data comparison.
