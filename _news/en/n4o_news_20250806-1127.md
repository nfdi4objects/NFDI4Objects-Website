The second **[Conference on Research Data Infrastructure (CoRDI)](https://www.nfdi.de/cordi-2025/)** will take place in Aachen from 26 to 28 August. The motto of this year's conference: "*RDM in action*"!

Members of NFDI4Objects will be represented with several contributions on the topics of RDM funding, data FAIRification, open data and a use case on FAIR data and linked open data.

Further information on NFDI4Objects at CoRDI can be found in the associated [activity](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20250804-1602/) on our website.
