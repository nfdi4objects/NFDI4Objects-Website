On **December 15, 2025,** the election of the spokesperson and deputy spokesperson for the NFDI4Objects consortium took place. The nomination phase for the election ended on October 27, 2025, with one nomination received for each position. 

The online vote resulted in the following unanimous outcome:

**Election result for the office of spokesperson**

* Dr. Philipp von Rummel (Secretary General at the German Archaeological Institute)

**Election result for the office of deputy spokesperson**

* Prof. Dr. Kai-Christian Bruhn (Professor of Interdisciplinary Applications of Spatial Information and Measurement Technology, Mainz University of Applied Sciences)



We warmly congratulate the elected (deputy) spokespersons on their election and would like to thank all participants for their commitment and for casting their votes.
