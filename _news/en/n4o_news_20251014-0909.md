The constituent meeting of the *TWG Provenance research in textile archaeology* will take place via Zoom on **7 November 2025 at 11.30 am**.

**Meeting link**

<https://uni-bonn.zoom-x.de/j/66664171913?pwd=Dp6lbiqqAQFLbacmRuctMdHyyOLSnJ.1>

The aim of the meeting is to formally establish the TWG and to discuss the organisational and content-related framework for further cooperation.

The proposal with topics and objectives of the TWG can be found on our website: <https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/twgs/provenienzforschung-im-bereich-textilarch%C3%A4ologie/>

The organisers look forward to your participation!
