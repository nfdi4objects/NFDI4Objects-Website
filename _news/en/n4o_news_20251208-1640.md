The meeting will take place online on Thursday, December 11, 2025, from 1:00 p.m. to 2:30 p.m. Registration is not necessary; you can simply join via the Zoom link: <https://hu-berlin.zoom-x.de/j/67288758009?pwd=xLbppQEuDOvqzyj9fEEJBYuUJw8Izc.1>.

We will present two now completed white papers from the NFDI4Objects Temporary Working Group (TWG) 2023.1 “Environment Analysis of the FDM Landscape in Museums and Collections” and open them up for comment:

the report on research data management (FDM) in museums and collections

* <https://community.nfdi4objects.net/assemblies/commons/f/1/proposals/44>
* <https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17776887>

the overall conclusion and recommendations of the TWG

* <https://community.nfdi4objects.net/assemblies/commons/f/1/proposals/45>
* <https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17778127>

The first link in each case leads to the NFDI4Objects Community Hub, where the documents can be commented on publicly. The second link leads to Zenodo, where the documents will be permanently available. You can already read the texts now; the option to comment will be set up in a few days.

The hosts are TWG Chairs Felix Schäfer, Johannes Schäffer, and Magdalene Schlösser, together with Frank von Hagel and Martin Stricker.

We therefore look forward to broad participation and will provide ample opportunity for discussion!
