We invite all interested parties to find out more about the work plan of the NFDI4Objects consortium initiative and to join in the discussion! We will use the event to present initial thoughts on the revision of the application and to take up suggestions, ideas and comments from the plenum. We will also explain the opportunities for further involvement in the content of the application process. We will shortly be distributing the programme on the usual social media and communication channels. 

We look forward to your active participation in the NFDI4Objects community meeting and your involvement in the further course of the application process and will of course be happy to answer any questions you may have!
