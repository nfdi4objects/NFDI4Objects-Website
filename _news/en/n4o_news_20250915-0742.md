This year's CAA Germany workshop will take place in Cologne from 25-26 September. This year's workshop is designed in particular - but not exclusively - to offer young researchers and students a platform to exchange ideas with others, learn from their experiences and build a network of contacts. In addition, new results and new approaches from all areas will be presented and discussed.

NFDI4Objects will be represented at the workshop with several practical examples.

The workshop is divided into the following topics:

*1. new research results and findings in the fields of archaeology and computer science*

*2. ongoing (BA, MA/MSC, PhD) theses and student research projects*

*3. short presentation of networking and collaboration opportunities*

Information on the workshop and the preliminary programme can be found [here on the CAA DE website](https://ag-caa.de/caa-de-workshop-koeln-2025/).

The workshop is organised by Lutz Schubert and Tom Noack from the Institute of Computer Science. The meeting will take place in [lecture theatre 112](https://verwaltung.uni-koeln.de/abteilung54/content/sachgebiet_4/lehrraeume/211___ibw_gebaeude/hoersaal_h_112/index_ger.html) on the university campus of the University of Cologne.
