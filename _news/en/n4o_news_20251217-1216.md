The Community Cluster “Protected Heritage Sites” invites all interested 
parties to its next meeting on Tuesday, January 13, 2026, from 10:00 
a.m. to 12:00 p.m. We are currently discussing a data model for the 
exchange of [protected heritage sites](https://osf.io/pwzg4/files/b4pjf).

The event will take place via Zoom.

Zoom-Link: <https://zoom.us/j/91094521138?pwd=zJLwXZetdBcpMpssA9RpZPVCRFUWDP.1>

Registration is not necessary.

Further information about the cluster can be found on the [portal](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc-26-denkmalfl%C3%A4chen-ma%C3%9Fnahmenfl%C3%A4chen-schutzfl%C3%A4chen/) and in the [OSF instance.](https://osf.io/pwzg4/overview)

We look forward to your participation!
