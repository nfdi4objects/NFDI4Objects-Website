From 26 to 28 August 2025, the 2nd Conference on Research Data Infrastructures took place at RWTH Aachen University. NFDI4Objects was also on site. The exchange around research data management, data, knowledge graphs, terminologies, subject-specific needs and infrastructures was very valuable and gave us many impulses and also use cases for cross-consortia cooperation.

A [short report summarising the contributions of NFDI4Objects at CoRDI](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20250804-1602/#looking-back) can be read in the related activity on our website.
