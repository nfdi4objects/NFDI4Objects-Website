NFDI4Objects is now offering regular **consultation hours for long-term archiving**. This will take place on the **last Tuesday of every month** and can also be arranged individually if required.

The consultation hour is open to everyone. General questions about file formats, documentation and possible automation as well as IANUS offers for long-term archiving can be discussed! If you have detailed questions about data or its organisation, please send a short email in advance to [ianus-fdz@dainst.de](mailto:ianus-fdz@dainst.de). 

Date: every last Tuesday of the month

Time: 10:00 a.m.

Access data for Zoom: <https://dainst-org.zoom.us/meeting/register/h7-uof3JSUaj9btuGdx7eg>

We look forward to the exchange!
