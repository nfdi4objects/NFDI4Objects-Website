NFDI4Objects is hosting an interactive webcast on research data management in collaboration with CAA Germany.

**Date: 14 November 2025**\
**Time: 2:00 p.m. – 4:00 p.m.**\
**Location: [Zoom](https://uni-koeln.zoom.us/j/98031938850?pwd=RHJNRmIrdit3WW1Mdks3SmZ3NWZ3Zz09)**

Submit your questions about research data management by **7 November** using **this [form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScayQDhspjaf5TMSoCdRLHMji3-mPvB5bi1Jx3n-TiCOHGnGQ/viewform) provided by CAA**.

In the interactive webcast, we will answer the questions submitted and talk to participants about research data management and the support options offered by NFDI4Objects.

Even if you don't have any questions of your own, it's still worth taking part: you'll find out what topics other colleagues are interested in and receive practical tips for working with your research data.

**Further information can be found in the** **corresponding [activity on our website](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20251014-1108/)!**
