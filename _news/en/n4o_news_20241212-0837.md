Dear NFDI4Objects Community, the date for our **3rd Community Meeting** has been set: Next year our conference will take place from **30 September to 2 October 2025** at the **German Mining-Museum in Bochum**. 

The meeting will provide an opportunity to discuss progress and challenges in the consortium and to network with members of the community.

More information will follow soon in the [corresponding activity](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20250324-0956/). Subscribe to our [newsletter ](https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/n4o-newsletter)to make sure you don't miss any updates.
