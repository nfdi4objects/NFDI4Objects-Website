Our consortium will once again be represented at DHd in 2026, which will take place at the University of Vienna this time. 

In collaboration with NFDI4Culture, NFDI4Memory, SODa, and digiS, members of our consortium will host the workshop “Collection Data as Research Data in the Digital Humanities” as part of the DHd AG “Collections as Data.” 

In addition, [TWG N4O Object Core Metadata Profile (OCMDP) & Material Cultural Heritage Crosswalk Ontology (MaCHeCO)](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/twgs/twg2024-1_omds_oo/) will present a poster on the current status of mapping work based on the conservation-restoration use case.
