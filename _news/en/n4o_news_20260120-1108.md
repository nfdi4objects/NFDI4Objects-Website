Controlled vocabularies and standard data play a central role in the reusability of object-describing data in museums and collections. [The minimum data set](https://wiki.deutsche-digitale-bibliothek.de/x/FoEtBw) recommendation for museums and collections already contains important recommendations on the use of terminology in core elements of object documentation. 

A sub-working group of the [Minimum Data Set Working Group](https://www.museumsbund.de/ag-minimaldatensatz/) of the Documentation Section of the German Museum Association (DMB) has therefore compiled a list of relevant vocabularies for collection documentation and suitable offerings for specific documentation focuses and disciplines. In addition to a brief description of the respective content focus and the technical implementation status of the web offerings, each is classified according to the criteria of the internationally recognized [FAIR principles.](https://www.go-fair.org/fair-principles/) This enables a well-founded assessment of the sustainability and reusability of the described vocabularies in the contexts of networked publishing and effective research data management.
