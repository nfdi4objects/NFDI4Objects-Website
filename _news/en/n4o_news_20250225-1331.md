On 14 February 2025, the [Temporary Working Group ‘Community standards for modelling fuzziness & wobbliness in research data using Semantic Web technologies and formalisms (FuzzyWobblySW)’](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/twgs/community-standards-for-modelling-fuzziness-wobbliness-in-research-data-using-semantic-web-technologies-and-formalisms-fuzzywobblysw/) was decided in the Steering Committee.

If you would like to actively participate in the TWG (advanced knowledge of semantic modelling in RDF is required), please subscribe to the [mailing list](https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/n4o_twg_fuzzy-wobbly-sw). Further information on the TWG can be found [here](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/twgs/community-standards-for-modelling-fuzziness-wobbliness-in-research-data-using-semantic-web-technologies-and-formalisms-fuzzywobblysw/).

Co-Chairs of the TWG are [Florian Thiery (LEIZA)](https://www.leiza.de/ueber-uns/team/person/florian-thiery) and [Karsten Tolle (Goethe University Frankfurt am Main)](http://www.bigdata.uni-frankfurt.de/tolle/).

The first ‘constituent meeting’ will be held on **18 March 2025, 14.00h - 15.30h via Zoom**. The link will be sent via the mailing list.

We look forward to a ‘linked’ collaboration 🙂.
