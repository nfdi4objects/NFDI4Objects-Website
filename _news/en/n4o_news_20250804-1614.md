From 19 to 20 May 2025, a workshop on digitisation, digital methods in teaching and research data management in archaeology took place at the Technical University of Darmstadt as part of the project ‘**[Digital Competences in the Archaeological Disciplines (DiKopA)](https://dikopa.net/)**’. NFDI4Objects was also represented by Kai-Christian Bruhn.

A **report on the workshop** can be found on the [DiKopA website](https://dikopa.net/2025/05/bericht-zweiter-workshop-des-dikopa-projekts/). Further information on the workshop can be found in the related [activity](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/activities/n4o_activity_20250513-1048/) on our website.
