We would like to draw your attention to the following dates from our network:

Would you like to publish your object data on the [German Digital Library (DDB)](https://www.deutsche-digitale-bibliothek.de/) portal, but don't know what steps are involved? Do you have questions about registration, the cooperation agreement, or the requirements for delivery data?                     

The German Digital Library (DDB) offers a consultation hour once a month.  This is aimed at all museum and collection staff who are interested in supplying data to the DDB. Old, new, and potential data partners are welcome.                   

This year's dates are: March 12, April 9, June 11, July 9, August 13, September 10, 2026, October 8, 2026, November 12, and December 10, 2026, from 1:30 p.m. to 2:30 p.m. each day       

Where: Online via  [Webex](https://spk-berlin.webex.com/spk-berlin/j.php?MTID=m5d8daa10921badcfd4b1d07e1116cc57)       

What: Questions can be submitted in advance via [Etherpad](https://yopad.eu/p/ddb-sprechstunde-365days)        

No registration required                  

We look forward to seeing you!              

Your German Digital Library Museum Department
