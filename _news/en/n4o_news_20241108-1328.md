Our **Community Cluster ‘[On-Site Documentation](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc-23-grabungsdokumentation/)’** would like to invite all interested parties to the next digital **cluster meeting** on **19 November 2024**. The meeting will take place online via Zoom from **5 to 6:30 pm**. 

Registration is not necessary.

[Link to the Zoom meeting](https://hs-mainz-de.zoom.us/j/61662169537?pwd=VTdqeXNyTTFCNVNxOERmOW4zUVlsdz09)

You are welcome to send your own **suggestions for the agenda** to **[Julian Hollaender](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/persons/julianhollaender/)** (Julian.Hollaender@rps.bwl.de) in advance. The provisional agenda will be made available online on our website in the run-up to the meeting.

All previous documents on the activities of the Community Cluster can be found in the [Open Science Framework](https://osf.io/esjtk/).  

We look forward to your active participation!
