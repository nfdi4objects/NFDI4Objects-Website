Thank you to the Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz for the exciting 3rd Open Science Festival in Mainz from 17–18 September 2024!

NFDI4Objects was represented at the joint NFDI e.V. stand, at the Ignite Talks and at the workshops on the topics of "Data Visualisation", "Working with Tools and Services" and "Linked Open Data", "From Data Collection to Publication" and "Challenges in Working in the Field of Digital Humanities".

Many thanks to our speakers Kai-Christian Bruhn, Florian Thiery, Lennart Linde, Julia Tolksdorf and Robert Zwick for the interesting insights into the various tools and services that NFDI4Objects has developed for and by our community.
We are already looking forward to the next time.
