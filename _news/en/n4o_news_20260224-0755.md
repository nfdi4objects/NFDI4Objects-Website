After a long break, the **Community Cluster Data Capture and Creation** is resuming its work and cordially invites all interested parties to participate.

The cluster focuses on issues relating to the entire data lifecycle – from **data generation and description** to **data provision** and **integration into other systems**.

The **cluster meetings** will take place **every second Tuesday every two months,** starting **on March 10, 2026,** **from 2:30 p.m. to 4:00 p.m.** Due to thematic overlaps, individual meetings will be held jointly with the **Community Cluster Authority Files and Community-driven Vocabularies.**

The following topics are planned for 2026:

* Location data
* Mapping terminologies with **Cocoda**
* Hands-on sessions and innovations at **DANTE**
* **3D data**
* **LIDO** and L**IDO for architecture and stationary works**

To stay up to date, we recommend [subscribing to the mailing list](https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/admin/n4o_cc_data_capture_creation) and [joining the cluster's RocketChat channel.](https://n4o-chat.nfdi.de/channel/CC_Data_Caption_and_Creation)

Further information on the Community Cluster Data Capture and Creation can be found on our [portal.](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/portal/ccs/cc2_data_capture_and_creation/)
