From March 6–8, 2026, the network **“Digital Competencies in Archaeology”** (DiKopA) is hosting a two-day hackathon on the topic of archaeogaming at Christian Albrecht University in Kiel. The hackathon will begin in the **afternoon of March 6, 2026, at the Institute for Prehistory and Early History** and end **at noon on March 8, 2026.** During the two afternoons and mornings, small groups will work on creating short archaeogames.

There are limited funds available to subsidize students who live further away.  Further information about the event can be found [on the network's website.](https://dikopa.net/2026/01/ankuendigung-archaeogaming-hackathon-kiel-6-8-3-2026/)
