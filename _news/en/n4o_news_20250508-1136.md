The first meeting of the newly founded TWG Community Recommendations for Long-Term Archiving will take place online via Zoom on June 4, 2025 from 10:00 am to 11:30 am.

The following link leads to the registration for the Zoom meeting: https://dainst-org.zoom.us/meeting/register/LUhWBiNWQ2O7084yOW1PDw

In the meeting, the necessary steps will be discussed in which form the update of the recommendations for long-term archiving can be approached. In particular, more information is needed on 3D data and geodata. Our community also repeatedly expresses a need for large (TIN) models. We would like to find a way to archive the current status with a DOI.

All information on the TWG can be found in the [portal](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/twgs/community-empfehlungen-zur-langzeitarchivierung/) of the N4O website.
