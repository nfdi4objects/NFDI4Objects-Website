The **[Terminology Services 4 NFDI](https://base4nfdi.de/projects/ts4nfdi)** (TS4NFDI) is a cross-domain service for the provision, curation, development, harmonisation and mapping of terminologies. It aims to facilitate consensus building and interoperability of services between different disciplines in order to create a common knowledge representation and a common knowledge development framework. This concerns the integration and convergence of individual solutions into a standardised, interoperable and sustainable service suite with service wrapper, API gateway, mapping service and reusable GUI widgets.

On **16 July 2025** at **1 pm**, Roman Baum, TS4NFDI/ZB Med, will present this central basic service of the NFDI as part of the Community Cluster "[Authority Files and community-driven Vocabularies](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc3_authority_and_norm_data/)".
