The first meeting of the GIS subgroup of the temporary working group ‘Community Recommendations for Long-Term Archiving’ will take place on 12 November, 11:30 a.m. - 1:00 p.m., via Zoom.  

Zoom link: [https://dainst-org.zoom.us/j/99778890745?pwd=mQN86sJuRHyFaN5mZFa8HFhZJ2zHW3.1](https://dainst-org.zoom.us/j/99778890745?pwd=mQN86sJuRHyFaN5mZFa8HFhZJ2zHW3.1)

We want to work together to define the points that the recommendations on GIS need to cover.

Colleagues who are interested in participating are very welcome.   

If you are interested, please feel free to subscribe to our mailing list: [https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/lza-empfehlungen](https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/lza-empfehlungen) or contact [Juliane.Watson@dainst.de](mailto:Juliane.Watson@dainst.de) directly.
