As part of our 3rd Community Meeting from 30 September to 2 October 2025 at the German Mining Museum Bochum, the **Calls for Ignite Talks and Posters will be extended until 22 September**. All information on the submissions and the Community Meeting can be found on our [event website](https://cfp.nfdi4objects.net/n4o-cm2025/cfp) and on our [website](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/activities/n4o_activity_20250930-0800/).

You can register for our Community Meeting via the following [link](https://reg.nfdi4objects.net/N4O/CM2025/).

We look forward to your contributions and participation!
