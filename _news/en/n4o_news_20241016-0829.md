On **22 October 2024** from **10:00 am to 3:30** pm, the NFDI4Objects **[Community Cluster "Objects as Inscription Carriers"](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc8_objects_as_information_carriers/)** cordially invites all interested parties to participate in the ***online workshop "The Materiality of Inscribed Cultural Property in Digital Space"***. 

The workshop will take place online via Webex at the following address: <https://spk-berlin.webex.com/meet/c.klose>

The full programme and more information about the event can be found [here](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20241016-0814/). Registration is not necessary.

We look forward to your participation!
