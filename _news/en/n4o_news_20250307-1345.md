The following Commons paper is now online: ‘Overview of research data management in museums and university collections’ by Magdalene Schlösser, Johannes, Schäffer, Frank von Hagel and Felix Falco Schäfer is the first paper adopted by the General Assembly. 

It analyses and describes the current situation of museum and university collections with regard to research data and infrastructures. The paper is available at this link: <https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.13362432>.
