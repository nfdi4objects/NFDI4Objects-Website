On **December 10, 2025, at 10 a.m**., the inaugural meeting of the new Community Cluster (CC) on the topic of **“Qualification and Open Educational Resources (OER)”** will take place online.

The planned cluster is aimed at anyone who is involved in teaching FDM skills in the field of material heritage of human history in their everyday professional life or plans to do so – whether in teaching, continuing education, or practice.

**Zoom link:** <https://htwk-leipzig.zoom-x.de/j/65767860147?pwd=FhotWbY94UDVvQLpoUcXaQVW2k5Roe.1> 

**Meeting ID:** 657 6786 0147

**Passcode:** 095395

We want to form interest groups for planned work packages and discuss how to proceed.

**Registration is not necessary. We look forward to your participation. Further information is available in the[ Community Hub.](<>)**
