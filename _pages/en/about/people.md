# NFDI4Objects
NFDI4Objects is a consortium of leading research institutions, museums, archives, and universities in the field of material culture and object research. Together, we are building a sustainable research data infrastructure for archaeology and object-related sciences.

Our interdisciplinary team of researchers, developers, and data experts develops standards, tools, and services for research data management – from data capture in the field to long-term archiving and reuse.
