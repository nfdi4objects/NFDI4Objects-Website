The Minimum Record Recommendation Working Group, together with the **LIDO Service Center (NFDI4Culture)** founded at the end of 2025, is introducing regular minimum data set consultation hours.

The **first consultation hour** will take place on February **18, 2026, from 2:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m**.

Are you interested in the minimum data set recommendation for museums and collections, but unsure how to integrate it into your documentation work? Do you have questions about individual data fields, recording instructions, or the recommended vocabularies?

 The consultation hour offers a space for exchange and is aimed at museum staff, software providers, digital researchers, and anyone who would like to use the recommendation in their work and has unanswered questions. We cordially invite you to participate.

Further dates in 2026 are:

**May 20, 2026**

**July 8, 2026**

**September 23, 2026**

**November 25, 2026**

**from 2:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m. each day**.

**Prior registration** is requested using this [form](https://nfdi4culture.de/de/helpdesk.html). Please select the **LIDO Service Center** when registering.
