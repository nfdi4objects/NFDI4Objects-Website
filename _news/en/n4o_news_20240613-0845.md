**First pitch event for Dataship candidates on 14 June 2024 from 9-11 a.m.**

On 14 June 2024, the time has come: the pitch event for our first call for proposals for the NFDI4Objects Dataship will take place online via Zoom from 9 to 11 am.

We would like to invite all interested parties to take part in the pitch event. The event is open to the public and will be held in English. A total of 10 candidates will present their submitted project concepts for the FAIRification of their existing datasets. The NFDI4Objects Steering Committee will then decide on the four winners.

Interested parties can use the following link to take part in the digital pitch event: https://dainst-org.zoom.us/j/99612404658?pwd=bjBYK3FsNSs4TVlvNFBucHBKV1lOdz09

We look forward to your participation!

Further information on the Dataship can be found in the related [activity](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20240701-0632/#pitch-event) on our website.
