We invite all interested parties to the 2nd meeting of the NFDI4Objects Community Cluster ‘Denkmal-, Maßnahmen- und Schutzflächen / Protected Heritage Sites’ on 2 April 2025 from 12:30-2:30 pm.

[Link to the Zoom meeting](https://dainst-org.zoom.us/j/93152008530?pwd=NkJ2MzVRU1IxaDl2WXBDVEdQSHcvZz09).

Information on the objectives of the cluster can be found on the [portal ](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/portal/ccs/cc-26-denkmalfl%C3%A4chen-ma%C3%9Fnahmenfl%C3%A4chen-schutzfl%C3%A4chen/)of the N4O website.

The topic of the meeting will be the drafts for data models for the areas of monuments/heritage sites, measures, protected areas and citizen science already presented in the constitutive meeting. We look forward to a lively exchange and your input. The drafts of the data models can be found on [OSF](https://osf.io/pwzg4/?view_only=).

Please feel free to invite other interested colleagues and distribute the invitation via your channels.       

If you would like to stay informed about the work of the cluster, please subscribe to the our [mailing list](https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/n4o_cc_protected_heritage_sites)!
