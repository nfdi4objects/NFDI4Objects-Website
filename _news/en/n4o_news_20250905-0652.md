The [Theories in Archaeology working group](https://www.agtida.de/) is focussing on the topic of ‘*Digitality and Archaeology*’ this year. The focus is on a critical and reflective examination of central questions and aspects relating to big/open data, open science, research data management, artificial intelligence, digital methods, etc. in archaeology. To this end, TidA is also working with NFDI4Objects, among others.

The kick-off event for the main topic is the fishbowl discussion "*Digital Minds or Digital Mines? Digitality and Archaeology*" on 9 September 2025 at the [conference of the Antiquities Associations in Frankfurt (Oder)](https://mova-online.de/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/MOVA_Programm_07_2025.pdf).

Fabian Fricke will take part in the discussion on behalf of NFDI4Objects. 

Further information can be found on the [TidA website](https://www.agtida.de/aktivitaeten-und-output/tagungen/schwerpunkt-digitalitaet-archaeologie/) and in the associated [activity](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20250903-0839/) on our website.
