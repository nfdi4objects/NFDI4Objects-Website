## Project Description

This research project addresses how to uniformly model uncertainty and imprecision in scientific data across different research domains. The focus is on numismatic and ceramic studies.

## Goal

Development of unified approaches for modeling uncertainty (fuzziness) and imprecision (wobbliness) in various research domains, with a special focus on numismatics and ceramic studies.

## Core Areas

The trail project encompasses two lifecycle phases:
- **Enrich & Interpret**: Enrichment and interpretation of data considering uncertainties
- **Reuse & Cite**: Reuse and citation of imprecise data

## Work Steps

1. **Collection and Evaluation**: Gathering existing modeling approaches to uncertainty across disciplines
2. **Case Study**: Creating a case study using collection-based numismatic and ceramic research
3. **Transformation Rules**: Developing rules for cross-disciplinary application
4. **Publication**: Publishing white papers and blue papers to establish best practices
5. **Education**: Integrating prototypical approaches into university curricula

## Task Area

This project is assigned to **Task Area 2: Collecting** and focuses on the enrichment and interpretation of collection data.
