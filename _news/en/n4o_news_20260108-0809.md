The next meeting of the TWG Community Standards for modeling fuzziness & wobbliness in research data using Semantic Web technologies and formalisms, or FuzzyWobblySW for short, will take place digitally via Zoom on **January 27, 2026, from 10:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m.**

The last meeting on December 2, 2025, produced several definitions, e.g., for vagueness, as well as a template for describing use cases and their corresponding representation in RDF.
