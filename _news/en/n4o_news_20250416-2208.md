Our consortium was represented with two workshops at the [annual conference of the Association for Digital Humanities in German-speaking countries](https://dhd2025.dig-hum.de/) in Bielefeld from 3-7 March. The four NFDI consortia from the humanities were available to answer questions at a joint stand.

Further information and a detailed report can be found in the related [activity](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20250221-1122/) on our website.
