NFDI proposals must be submitted to the DFG Head Office in PDF format via the DFG proposal portal elan by November 2, 2021. More information: DFG – Nationale Forschungsdateninfrastruktur (https://www.dfg.de/foerderung/programme/nfdi/formulare_merkblaetter/index.jsp).

Further information on our application process can be found in the [related activity on our website](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20210203-0838/#deadline-for-the-submission-of-nfdi-applications-in-the-third-round).
