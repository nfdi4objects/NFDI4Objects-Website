Next year, the CAA International conference will take place in Athens from 5 to 9 May 2025. 

**NFDI4Objects** is represented by ***Session 17 "Comparing the Incomparable: Managing and Analysing Data from Heterogeneous Sources in Archaeological Research"***. The session will be chaired by [Fabian Riebschläger](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/portal/persons/fabianriebschl%C3%A4ger/) (German Archaeological Institute), Lisa Steinmann (German Archaeological Institute) and [Julian Hollaender](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/portal/persons/julianhollaender/) (State Office for Monument Preservation Baden-Württemberg).

The session is related to the Temporary Working Group “[Object- and geodata in the primary documentation](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/twgs/sach-und-geodaten-in-der-prim%C3%A4rdokumentation/)" and the Community Cluster “[On-site Documentation](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc-23-grabungsdokumentation/)”.

All information on the content of the session can be found [here](https://2025.caaconference.org/call-for-papers/session-list/#S17).

**Abstracts for papers and posters for individual sessions** can still be submitted until **24 November 2024**. [Here you can find the Call for Papers](https://2025.caaconference.org/call-for-papers/). We look forward to your numerous submissions.
