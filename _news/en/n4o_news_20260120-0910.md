The Community Cluster Protected Heritage Sites invites all interested parties to its next meeting **on Wednesday, February 11, 2026, from 10:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.** 

We are nearing the end of our discussion on a data model for the exchange of measure objects and are expected to begin discussing a data model for protected objects.

The event will take place via **[Zoom](https://zoom.us/j/91094521138?pwd=zJLwXZetdBcpMpssA9RpZPVCRFUWDP.1).**   

Registration is not necessary.

Further information about the cluster can be found on [OSF](https://osf.io/pwzg4/overview). 

We look forward to your participation!
