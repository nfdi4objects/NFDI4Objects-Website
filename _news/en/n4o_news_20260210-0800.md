**On February 19, 2026, starting at 1 p.m.**, the NFDI Consortium Text+ will host its Show & Tell event. Marthe Küster, supervised by Anja Gerber (KSW, TA6 NFDI4Objects), will present her master's thesis entitled “The World in Letters: Challenges in Indexing Historical Geodata in Digital Editions” online. 

" The collection of location data is indispensable when working with digital cultural heritage. This also includes the field of digital letter editions. In addition to the location information in letter metadata, which is usually entered manually as standard, digital methods and tools open up new possibilities for automatically collecting, tagging, and analyzing location data in letter texts." Küster, M. (2026).
