The Community Cluster ‘Public Archaeology’ was founded as a professional committee to bring together and network activists and administrators from the field of citizen science as well as scientists. It aims to contribute to the better integration of public archaeology into archaeological practice and research, to increase the visibility and scientific usability of citizen science data, and to strengthen the involvement of actors working in this field.

Further information about the CC can be found on the NFDI4Objects [portal](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc7_citizen_science_and_community_hubs/).

If you are interested in participating, you can join the [mailing list](https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/n4o-community-cluster-public-archaeology).
