The ***AG Digitalisierung of the [Dachverband Archäologischer Studierendenvertretungen e.V. (DASV e.V.)](https://www.dasv-ev.org/)*** is offering a QGIS workshop series this year.

Organised by two students, the first virtual meeting took place on 31 January 2025. The topic was a basic introduction to QGIS and the processing of a first small project.

The event was recorded and is available on YouTube:

In terms of content, the workshop is again aimed at beginners, whereby **you can contribute to the selection of topics until 20 July**. A [Google form is available for this purpose](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScQX3-W_pXePzIeVGBFqA6Z8yC0VyYn6z3JqCxdBq5sf_4O9g/viewform?usp=dialog).

After the workshop there will be an open plenary session for questions.

No previous knowledge is required for the workshop!

We use the QGIS version 3.40 “Bratislava”.

The DASV e.V. is an association of student councils, professional associations and other representatives of archaeological study programmes from all over Germany, Austria and Switzerland.
