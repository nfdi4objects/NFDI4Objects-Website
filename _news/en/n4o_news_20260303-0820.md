On February 13, 2026, the Steering Committee NFDI4Objects decided to establish the [TWG VocFlow. Workflows for the publication and maintenance of controlled vocabularies. ](https://community.nfdi4objects.net/assemblies/TWG/f/4/proposals/54)

The task of this TWG is to clarify **organizational processes** that arise during the publication and maintenance of already created vocabularies, e.g.:

* How are existing vocabularies prepared for publication?
* What steps and roles are necessary for this?
* How can teams be coordinated and long-term maintenance ensured? 

Best practice workflows are to be developed based on **three practical examples** from the consortium. The end result will be a **white paper in workbook format** that provides guidance and decision-making support. 

You can subscribe to the mailing list here: [https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/n4o_twg_vocflow. ](https://www.listserv.dfn.de/sympa/info/n4o_twg_vocflow)
