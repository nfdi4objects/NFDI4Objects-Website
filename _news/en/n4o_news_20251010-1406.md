The TWG ‘[Services and Tools for Research Collections and Provenance Research](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/twgs/dienste-und-tools-f%C3%BCr-forschungssammlungen-und-provenienzforschung/)’ of the Community Cluster [Digital Provenance Research](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc5_provenance_research/) of NFDI4Objects, together with SODa, invites you to a workshop discussion on the topic of **resources, services and tools for provenance research**!

Over the past few months, the TWG has compiled a **collection of links** that bundles resources, digital applications and services for provenance research across different topics and contexts. This has provided a status quo of current digital resources in provenance research. **User interfaces** have been developed for both NFDI4Objects and SODa in order to provide beginners or career changers in provenance research with a compact overview to get started. 

In a joint workshop report on **Wednesday, 15 October 2025 from 1 to 2 pm**, Angela Berthold (Münzkabinett, SMB / NFDI4Objects) and Gabriele Zöllner (SODa) will report on the work in the Temporary Working Group and the resulting applications.

**Registration is not required!** 

Link to dial in: <https://sammlungen.io/join> 

We look forward to your participation!
