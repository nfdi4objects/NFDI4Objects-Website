The Community Cluster ‘Denkmal-, Maßnahmen- und Schutzflächen / Protected Heritage Sites’ invites all interested parties to the next meeting on **Monday, 22 August 2025 from 11:00-13:00 online via Zoom**. In this meeting we will further discuss the topic ‘**Dating**’ and possibly continue working on the Citizen Science Standard. Everyone is cordially invited!

[Zoom meeting](https://zoom.us/j/91094521138?pwd=zJLwXZetdBcpMpssA9RpZPVCRFUWDP.1)

Registration is not necessary.

Further information on the cluster can be found in our [portal.](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/portal/ccs/cc-26-denkmalfl%C3%A4chen-ma%C3%9Fnahmenfl%C3%A4chen-schutzfl%C3%A4chen/)

We look forward to your participation!
