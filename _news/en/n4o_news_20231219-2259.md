We are leaving Twitter/X in favour of platforms that are currently better suited to our values. Find us on **Mastodon, LinkedIn** or **BlueSky**!

The NFDI Association and all NFDI consortia are committed to the core values of Open Science. We value diversity - not only in the data we care about, but also in the conversations we participate in. We are committed to creating an environment that is welcoming, supportive, respectful and free from threat, intimidation, bullying and discrimination. Twitter/X's decision to significantly reduce the number of content moderation teams and invite previously banned authors of hate messages back onto their platform is inconsistent with this commitment. We have therefore decided to adjust our social media strategy and distance ourselves from Twitter/X. From now on, we will focus our attention on our platforms that currently correspond better with our values, such as our own friendly, community-moderated NFDI Mastodon instance, LinkedIn, BlueSky or Instagram.

We will no longer use Twitter/X from 31 December 2023. Our account will remain inactive and serve as an archive rather than a communication channel. We invite you to follow us on other platforms that support constructive and respectful dialogue.

You can find us on these platforms from now on:

* https://www.instagram.com/nfdi4objects/
* https://www.linkedin.com/company/nfdi4objects/
* https://bsky.app/profile/nfdi4objects.bsky.social
* https://nfdi.social/@nfdi4objects
