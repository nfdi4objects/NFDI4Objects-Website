The conference **FORGE25** - *Research Data in the Humanities* - will take place this year from **24 to 26 September** at the **University of Rostock**. The theme of this year's conference is "*Rethinking data*". **Registration** is still possible until **15 September**.

NFDI4Objects will be represented at FORGE with several talks, workshops and poster contributions. All **information** can be found here in the [related activity](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20250804-1651/) on our website.
