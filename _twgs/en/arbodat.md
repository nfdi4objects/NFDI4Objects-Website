## Description

This TWG is still in preparation. 


Here you will soon find all information about the TWG . If you are curious and interested in participating in our TWG, please contact the staff of [Task Area 3](https://n4o.pages.gitlab.rlp.net/website/portal/tas/ta3/).

## Focus

The focus is on standardized metadata for instrument configurations in analytical methods such as:
- Optical microscopy
- X-ray fluorescence
- Raman spectroscopy


## Goals

The goal is to develop a metadata profile for instrument parameters that are significant for data comparison.
