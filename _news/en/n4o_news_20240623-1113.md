September is the month: The second NFDI4Objects Community Meeting will take place from 25 to 27 September 2024 in Mainz at the Leibniz Centre for Archaeology (LEIZA) and Mainz University of Applied Sciences (HS Mainz)! We are pleased to present a varied programme on the topic of research data management of object-related data and the FAIR, TRUST and CARE principles.

There is no participation fee for our Community Meeting. You can [register](https://reg.nfdi4objects.net/N4O/CM2024/) here. We look forward to your participation!

The **detailed programme** with information on location and room can be found **[here](https://cfp.nfdi4objects.net/N4O-CM2024/)**. Please note that there may be changes to the programme at short notice. You can also use this tool to send in submissions for the Call for Posters and Call for Ignite Talkers at a later date.

**[All information about the 2nd NFDI4Objects Community Meeting this year in Mainz can be found here.](https://www.nfdi4objects.net/en/activities/n4o_activity_20240806-1114/)**
