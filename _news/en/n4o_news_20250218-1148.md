The digital capture, management, and publication of research data are playing an increasingly important role in conservation and restoration. But how are conservation and restoration data—whether from specific object treatments or conservation science research projects—currently being collected and utilized? What challenges exist in practice? The TWG "Environmental Analysis of the FDM Landscape in the Field of Preservation and Care of Cultural Heritage" is addressing these questions and wants to ensure that our work program in Task Area 4 is practical and relevant. For this, we need your support!

Please participate in our survey by **March 17, 2025**, and help us gain a better understanding of the current state of research data management in the various restoration disciplines. Your feedback is crucial for effectively directing future activities in Task Area 4.

Here is the link to the survey: [[Link to the survey](https://survey.nfdi4objects.net/index.php/518788?lang=de)]

Participation takes about 15-20 minutes. We look forward to your perspectives and sincerely thank you for your support!
