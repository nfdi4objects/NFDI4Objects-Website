## About NFDI4Objects Commons

The **NFDI4Objects Commons** represent the **proposals, recommendations and standards of our community** and thus document **best practices, technical descriptions, open educational resources and recommendations for the development of services**. The Commons thus provide constantly updated access to the results of the consortium's work.

## Standardisation Process

On the way from proposal to becoming part of the Commons, resources go through a **participatory and collaborative standardisation process**. The knowledge of the community is thus transferred into the consortium's standards.
